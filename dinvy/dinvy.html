YOU HAVE BILLING OPTIONS

END CURRENCY HEADACHES FOR GOOD

Collect and manage payments without stressing over the complexities that come with billing clients in multiple currencies. Locations, divisions, acquisitions, foreign subsidiaries of your organization – master them all stress-free with Dinvy.

  • Get unlimited billing entities with entity ID (EIN, registration number), address, taxation, remittance bank information, and many more.

  • Multi-currency support with defaults per client, and flexibility to define currency per project – allowing bill rate and cost rate by person/role in all supported currencies.

  • Never go over budget again. View real-time daily usage reports to see how many hours teams have worked, and get daily updates to stay in the know.

  • A/R and invoice management across all your billing entities in one location. Use filters and granular searching capabilities to narrow down results to specific units.
